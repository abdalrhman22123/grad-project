What screen sizes are available for car displays?

We offer a wide range of screen sizes from 7 inches to 12.3 inches. The most popular sizes are 9 inches and 10.1 inches, which fit most standard vehicle dashboards. Custom sizes can also be arranged for specific vehicle models.

2
Features

Are the car screens compatible with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto?

Yes, all our premium car screens come with built-in Apple CarPlay and Android Auto support. This allows seamless integration with your smartphone for navigation, music, calls, and messaging while keeping your eyes on the road.

3
Installation

How difficult is the installation process?

Installation difficulty varies depending on your vehicle model. Most of our screens are plug-and-play and can be installed in 30-60 minutes with basic tools. We provide detailed installation guides and video tutorials. Professional installation is recommended for complex setups.

4
Features

Do car screens work with backup cameras?

Absolutely! All our car screens support backup camera integration. They automatically switch to the rear camera view when you shift into reverse. The screens support both wired and wireless backup camera connections.

5
Support

What is the warranty period for car screens?

We offer a comprehensive 2-year warranty on all car screens, covering manufacturing defects and hardware malfunctions. Extended warranty options are available for purchase. The warranty includes free technical support throughout the coverage period.

6
Features

Can I use GPS navigation without a phone connection?

Yes, many of our advanced models come with built-in GPS modules and offline map capabilities. However, for real-time traffic updates and the most current maps, we recommend connecting your smartphone via CarPlay or Android Auto.

7
Products

Are the screens readable in direct sunlight?

Our car screens feature high-brightness displays (400-1000 nits) with anti-glare coatings, making them easily readable even in direct sunlight. The brightness automatically adjusts based on ambient light conditions for optimal visibility.

8
Features

Do the screens support steering wheel controls?

Yes, our screens are compatible with most factory steering wheel controls. This allows you to control volume, skip tracks, answer calls, and navigate menus without taking your hands off the wheel, ensuring safer driving.

9
Features

What audio features are included?

Our car screens include built-in amplifiers, equalizers, and support for various audio formats including FLAC, MP3, and WAV. They feature multiple audio outputs, Bluetooth audio streaming, and compatibility with factory amplifiers and subwoofers.

10
Support

How do I update the software on my car screen?

Software updates can be performed via USB drive or over-the-air (OTA) updates on Wi-Fi enabled models. We release regular updates to improve performance, add features, and ensure compatibility with the latest smartphones. Update notifications appear automatically on the screen.
